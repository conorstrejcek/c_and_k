Hello there from routrr,

            When we started routrr back in September 2011, we had hoped that it would be able to grow into something that students could use to get the best deal on the most convenient bus trips home.  We needed help from all of you and you gave it to us: sending us feedback on our initial site design, helping us make something that really didn't suck, and assuring us that ours was an idea you were interested in and would use. 

            This summer, we started reaching out to Peoria Charter, Suburban Express, and LEX to talk to them about partnering with us to provide you with bus trips you could actually take.  We told them all about how psyched you were to use our site and we thought we made it pretty clear that working with us would be good for them and you at the same time.  However, after repeated attempts to contact them, we were either ignored or threatened with lawsuits.  We've been thinking about various ways that wouldn't necessarily involve working with the companies, but there's only so much we can do from our end. 
          
            To this end, we're shutting down routrr as you and I know it.  We've learned a ton throughout this process and we have you to thank for it.  Rest assured, we'll be back with something new soon, but until then, we wish you the best.
 
Regards,
Keanan and Conor
