About Us



     Once upon a time, Keanan was looking for a way to get home. He had tickets to a concert downtown Chicago. The problem was that he had to be on campus until 2 and the concert started at 7. So, Keanan went online and pulled up websites for three different bus companies. After half an hour of scrolling and searching, he still hadn't found a trip that would work with his schedule. He thought, "Isn't there a way that I can search trips from all these companies on one site?" After realizing there wasn't, he pitched his idea for a "College Bus Trip Search Engine" to Conor and routrr was born. They started building the site in September 2011 and worked around their class schedule to find time to code in Suite 2F.

     We've come a long way (we still have the original legal pad sketches on the wall) but we're always looking for new ideas and ways to improve. We'd love to hear how you're using routrr and we encourage you to check out our "Suggestion Box" or send either of us an email. We read every email that we receive and try to respond to as many as we can. We hope to hear from you soon and thank you again for using routrr!
